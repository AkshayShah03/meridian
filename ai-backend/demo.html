Meridian

The reference point for operational intelligence.

A trading desk operations team was working a Friday afternoon when a counterparty dispute required them to cross-reference a regulatory deadline, pull the latest central bank statement, verify a notional figure, and confirm a wire transfer amount — all within a 12-minute window before the market closed.

Four browser tabs. Three apps. Two phone calls. They missed the window by four minutes.

Meridian routes every factual query to its authoritative source automatically — news to live feeds, calculations to a verified engine, time to the actual timezone database — and streams the answer back in real time. No switching. No waiting. No hallucinations on anything that can be verified.

Memory service — Three storage layers per user. Short-term: Redis list per session, 24-hour TTL. Semantic: pgvector embeddings (nomic-embed-text, 768 dimensions) retrieved by cosine similarity. Episodic: Extractive summary generated automatically after 20 turns.
